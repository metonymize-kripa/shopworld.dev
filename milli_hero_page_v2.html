How it works

It learns from your team. Then it runs like software.

milli.run studies your real tickets, macros, and policies and turns the work your team already does into fast, dependable software. The result handles the routine flood the same way every time — and hands anything unfamiliar or risky straight back to a person.

Learns — in the background

Your playbook, turned into software.

It reads your support history and policies, learns how your best agents resolve each request, and turns those workflows into rules it can run. Every escalation it sees makes the next version better — no rip-and-replace, no months of tuning.

Runs — on every request

Fast, consistent, and safe by default.

Live requests run as dependable software: it understands the ask, checks your policy, refuses unsafe actions, takes the right one, and writes a record of what it did. No guessing, no drift, and nothing it isn’t cleared to do.

How we prove it

The hardest moment in support isn’t the routine — it’s the fraud and abuse cases.

A capable AI assistant can carry on a friendly support conversation. The danger is what it actually does on the cases where money and brand are on the line — a customer making a chargeback or legal threat, a refund outside policy, a cancellation on an order that already shipped. Left to smooth things over, an unguarded model tends to give in: refund first, ask later. milli.run is built so the routine runs instantly and the risky moments stop for a rule check or a human.

The risk we target

Unguarded agents give money away to pressure.

The expensive failure pattern is consistent across the industry: under a threat or an insistent customer, a general model issues a refund or reverses a charge to de-escalate — skipping the policy check and never flagging possible fraud. It’s fluent and helpful right up until the moment that costs you.

How milli.run prevents it

It checks the rules first — and shows its work.

Before any refund, cancel, or address change, milli.run checks your policy and the order’s real status, routes fraud and abuse to a person, and writes an audit entry with the reason and a one-click undo. The routine still resolves in milliseconds; only the risky moments slow down — on purpose.

On the numbers: we run milli.run inside a simulated store that recreates these exact situations, and we’re completing a head-to-head against leading frontier models on an expanded, independent scenario set before we publish figures. We’d rather show you the full results — including where a general model does well — than a single number that looks too good to question. Benefits

Five reasons merchants choose milli.run.

Here’s what milli.run’s design means for your store.

01 · Speed

Answers in milliseconds.

milli.run resolves a learned request in a few thousandths of a second. In a product-search test, that was over 1,000× faster than a general model doing the same job — so customers get instant help and your queue never backs up.

02 · Learnability

Trained on your store, not generic data.

It learns from your own tickets, macros, and policies, so it sounds like you and follows your rules from day one. Every escalation teaches it more — with no costly retraining project.

03 · Auditability

Every action, on the record.

Each decision comes with the reason behind it and a one-click undo. Your operations, finance, and risk teams can see exactly what happened — no engineer required.

04 · Reliability

The same right answer, every time.

Because covered workflows run as fixed rules, not a fresh guess each time, milli.run follows your policy consistently — blocking unsafe actions and escalating fraud and abuse by design, not by best effort. No off-days, no drift.

Automate the routine 80%.
