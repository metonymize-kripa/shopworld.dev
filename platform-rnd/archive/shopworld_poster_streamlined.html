ShopWorld

Can an AI safely run meaningful parts of a Shopify merchant business before it receives live write access?

https://shopworld.dev/

ShopWorld is a resettable, stateful benchmark for AI store managers operating inside a simulated Shopify business.

The agent is evaluated across customer support, inventory, fulfillment, catalog operations, promotions, supplier coordination, advertising, and Shopify Admin GraphQL actions. The purpose is not tool-call correctness alone; it is safe operating judgment under merchant policy and delayed business consequences.

World

Visible state

  • Products, variants, collections, inventory, locations.
  • Orders, fulfillments, refunds, discounts, customers.
  • Support tickets, supplier POs, cash ledger, policies.

Hidden state

  • Customer patience, satisfaction, LTV, fraud risk.
  • Supplier reliability, lead time, defect rates.
  • Demand shocks, cash pressure, promo fatigue.

Actor simulators

Customers, shoppers, suppliers, logistics providers, advertising venues, and a merchant policy supervisor update the world between agent actions.

Evaluation

Primary loop

observe → act/message → mutate state → advance time → generate events → score outcome

Scoring target

Success requires task completion, policy compliance, API competence, business impact, customer outcome, and no collateral damage.

Readiness output

  • Unsafe permissions and representative failure traces.
  • Workflow-level scores by operational domain.
  • Recommended authority level: read-only, draft, supervised, or bounded automation.
